# Aushe Properties — Website Project

## Overview

Website for **Aushe Properties**, a luxury real estate development company specializing in modern residential properties in Harare, Zimbabwe. The founder is **Aubrey** (friend of Ashirbad). The company targets both local buyers and diaspora investors looking to buy land or build homes in Zimbabwe.

**Current website:** ausheproperties.com (basic Wix/template site — being replaced with a custom Next.js build)
**Repository:** https://github.com/r90ghosh/ausheproperties

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router) + TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui
- **Deployment:** Netlify (or Vercel)
- **Images:** Local `/images` folder (property photos from Google Drive "Aushe : AMG")
- **No backend/database needed initially** — static/marketing site with contact forms
